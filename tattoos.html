Artist Showcase

Matt Politte

Get To Know Matt!

Meet the owner Matt Politte! He is a two-time award-winning artist who travels the United States tattooing at conventions. He is mainly known for his work in St.Francois County. He has been tattooing for 12 years. Working in multiple shops through out the years and opened his shop Immortal Keep Tattoo & Fine Arts in 2023.

Chloe Kelly

Get To Know Chloe!

Chloe Kelly is one of Immortal Keep's newest artists but has quickly shown everyone her skills and passion for the Tattoo Industry. Chloe became a Licensed Tattoo artist in July of 2024 and has already provided a wide range of skills in her portfolio.

Johnathan Fox

Get To Know John!

Johnathan Fox has been with Immortal Keep since it opened in 2023 making sure that every client that came in left with good memories and their new favorite tattoo. John has been tattooing for the past six years staying in the St.Francois County Area. John's favorite tattoo style is traditional but has shown a wide range of skills with his tattoos in different categories.
